# GVHS NHS Website Demo Script

**Audience:** New NHS Members
**Duration:** 3-5 minutes
**Focus:** Balanced coverage of all user features

---

## INTRO (30 seconds)

> "Welcome to the GVHS National Honor Society website! This is your hub for everything NHS - announcements, volunteering opportunities, tutoring, and more. Let me walk you through how you'll be using this site."

---

## 1. REGISTRATION (45 seconds)

Navigate to: **Register page** (click "Sign In" → "Create an account" or go to `/register`)

**Key points to mention:**

- "First, you'll create your account"
- **USER ID:** "You'll create a 6-digit User ID - this does NOT need to be your school ID. Pick any 6 numbers you'll remember, like a PIN."
- **SECURITY:** "Your data is completely encrypted and secure - your password and personal information are hidden and protected."
- Fill in: First name, last name, optional email (for password recovery), password
- "Once registered, you're ready to go!"

---

## 2. HOME PAGE (45 seconds)

Navigate to: **Home** (`/`)

**Walk through:**

1. **Announcements Banner** - "Check here for important updates from NHS. Urgent ones appear in red, important in yellow."
2. **Four Pillars** - "Quick reminder of what NHS stands for: Scholarship, Service, Leadership, Character"
3. **Meeting Schedule** - "Find when and where our chapter meetings are held"
4. **Member Requirements** - "Your monthly requirements at a glance - meetings, service projects, GPA, dues"

---

## 3. VOLUNTEERING (45 seconds)

Navigate to: **Volunteering** (`/volunteering`)

**Walk through:**

1. "This is where you find volunteer opportunities"
2. **Partner Organizations** - "We partner with several organizations - NHS Elementary School Visits, Kids in Motion, Tech Seniors, and more"
3. **Express Interest** - "Click the heart icon to express interest in an opportunity. Fill out the form and we'll connect you."
4. **Suggest New Opportunity** - "Have a volunteer idea? Click here to suggest it to the admin team."

---

## 4. YOUR PROFILE (30 seconds)

Navigate to: **Profile** (`/tutor/profile`)

**Walk through:**

1. "This is your personal profile"
2. **Edit Info** - "Update your name or email anytime"
3. **Tutoring Subjects** - "Select subjects you're willing to tutor in - this helps other students find you"
4. **Highlighted Subjects** - "Pick 2-3 subjects you're best at to feature prominently"
5. **Stats** - "Track your tutoring hours and sessions"

---

## 5. TUTORING CHECK-IN (30 seconds)

Navigate to: **Tutor Status** (`/tutor/status`)

**Walk through:**

1. "This shows who's currently in the library available to tutor"
2. "When you're tutoring in the library, you'll check in using your User ID"
3. "Your name and highlighted subjects will appear here so students know who can help with what"
4. "The system auto-checks you out at the end of each period"

---

## WRAP-UP (15 seconds)

> "That's the essentials! Remember:
> - Check announcements on the home page
> - Find volunteer opportunities under Volunteering
> - Set up your tutoring subjects in your Profile
> - Questions? Contact Dr. Morabito"

---
